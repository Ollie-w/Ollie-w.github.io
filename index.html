The Tools

Development

My primary goal is to make beautiful websites that are responsive, adaptive and accessible with easily-maintainable code. With a design-background I have a particular affinity for CSS. I am also more than comfortable with vanilla javascript as well as deploying and utilising various js libraries when needed.

Design

I design websites and mobile apps, among other things. I have recently started using Sketch, which is great for UI/UX. Adobe Creative Cloud is still an important part of what I do. Typography, branding, layout and logo creation - all aspects of graphic design are important to me. I select the best tool for the task at hand. I also have extensive experience with photoshop image compositing.

Motion

I have animated the opening titles of BBC's Line of Duty, made in-show graphics for Sky Art's My Failed Novel, animated title cards for The Food Network’s Pioneer Woman and worked on pitches for ITV’s Unforgotten, ABC’s Of King’s and Prophets, BBC's The Living and the Dead and Jericho. I have experience using a wide variety of plugins including Nodes 2, Plexus 2 and the Trapcode Suite. I have dabbled with Cinema 4D and Nuke but After Effects is where my time and passion has primarily laid. I have experience of editing with both Premiere and Final Cut.
